Vancouver, British Columbia: Day 3

After two long days of exploration, I needed a bit of time to rest in Vancouver. Today was filled with colorful coffee shops, delicious dim sum, bizarre Japanese snacks, plastic Canadian "money", and a trip to British Columbia's only... Twin Peaks themed bar? In all honesty, this part of the trip was purely for my travel buddy Jamie. I've never seen the series but she proceeded to read aloud every drink on the menu while shrieking. I suppose that means it was pretty well done? Fortunately, they had a projector which was playing The Shining for those of us accompanying friends. I'm starting to feel at home here.
